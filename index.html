About

I'm a recent college graduate with a passion for data science and a relentless curiosity to explore the world through data. Throughout my academic journey, I discovered the power of storytelling through numbers and developed a deep love for learning new methods to unravel complex problems. My portfolio showcases a range of data science projects, each driven by my fascination with understanding patterns, making predictions, and deriving insights from data. I'm excited to connect with like-minded professionals and explore opportunities where I can apply my skills, learn, and make a difference!
